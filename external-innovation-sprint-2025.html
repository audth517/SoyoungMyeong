2025 Climate-Tech Innovation Sprint, KAIST-Ehwa
Soyoung Myeong
2025

2025 Climate-Tech Innovation Sprint is a team-based competition where students from KAIST and Ewha Womans University define climate-related challenges, develop solutions, and are evaluated for awards.

Using design templates provided by the Asan Foundation, I designed posters and banners in close collaboration with faculty members from KAIST and Ewha Womans University. The visual outcomes were carefully aligned with the foundation’s established identity while adapting the designs to an academic event context.
