Handheld Product (Hair-Dryer)
Super Dry
Soyoung Myeong
2025

Handheld Product is a project to design a hair dryer by combining geometric primitives and tangential organic surfaces.

The interior of the hair dryer is made of polyurethane foam, while the exterior is finished with resin and putty. The model features an acrylic end cap, and the final finish includes spray white paint, Max-Labo, and a power cord.

I focused on ensuring that the dryer fits comfortably in one hand. A distinctive feature was located between the fan housing and the handle. In particular, I paid close attention to the tangential lines at this intersection.
