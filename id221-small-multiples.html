Small Multiples
Twelve Months, Twelve Memories
Soyoung Myeong
2025

Small Multiples is a project inspired by Edward Tufte’s Wine Glasses, which explores a single theme through twelve graphic variations.

Twelve Months, Twelve Memories represents twelve major commemorative days of Korea, one for each month, expressed in the form of Christmas seals. Traditional Korean hanji paper is used as the background to emphasize cultural identity.

Christmas seals are tokens issued around Christmas to raise funds for tuberculosis prevention. This project draws from a childhood memory of collectively purchasing Christmas seals at school every year. Based on this nostalgia, the design concept presents Christmas seals issued this Christmas for use throughout 2026.
