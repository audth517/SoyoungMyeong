ICISTS Hackafair 2024
Soyoung Myeong, Somin Lee
2024

ICISTS Hackafair 2024 is an event that provides a platform for university students to develop creative ideas addressing social division. Hackafair combines a hackathon and a fair, where participants propose business models to tackle selected social divides and present their outcomes through booth-based exhibitions.

The poster focuses on the visual connection between two separated objects. The point where the objects meet represents the dot between “Hacka” and “fair,” expressing the fusion of the two words and the convergence of ideas.
