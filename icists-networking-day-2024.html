ICISTS Networking Day 2024
Soyoung Myeong(Poster, Leaflet), Somin Lee(Poster)
2024

ICISTS Networking Day is hosted for KAIST freshmen, offering an in-depth introduction to the organization and creating opportunities for networking through a shared meal.

The poster uses three-dimensional objects and typography to create visual depth. The QR code was intentionally tilted, prompting curiosity and engagement, often leading students to question whether it was scannable and encouraging them to interact with the poster more closely.
