Failed Tasks Festival 2025, CAF-ICISTS
Soyoung Myeong
2025

Failed Tasks Festival 2025 is the third edition of the event since 2023, serving as a fair where individuals share personal stories about the beauty of failure.

The design features a roly-poly toy as the main visual element, symbolizing resilience and the act of rising again after falling. Bold, eye-catching colors were used to emphasize this theme and create a strong visual presence.
