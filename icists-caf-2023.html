Failed Tasks Showdown Contest 2023, CAF-ICISTS
Soyoung Myeong, Somin Lee
2023

Failed Tasks Showdown Contest is a stand-up comedy–style event where participants share their experiences of failure, co-hosted by the Center for Ambitious Failure (CAF) and ICISTS.

Since speaking about failure in front of an audience can be intimidating, the posters were designed to feel lighthearted and approachable. One poster visually emphasizes the stand-up comedy concept, while the other explores a more playful typographic composition, reframing failure as something shareable, humorous, and open to conversation.
