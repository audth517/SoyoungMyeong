Failed Tasks Festival 2024, CAF-ICISTS
Soyoung Myeong
2024

Failed Tasks Festival 2024 is the second edition of the event following its debut in 2023, providing a space for individuals to share personal stories of frustration, failure, and overcoming challenges.

The poster adopts a subway station sign concept. It presents a sequence of stations from Frustration to the current station of Failure, where stories are shared, and onward to Overcoming framing failure as a transitional moment within a journey.
