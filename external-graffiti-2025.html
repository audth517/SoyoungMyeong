GRAFFITI 2025 is an ideathon that explores new ways of addressing social issues through AI startup technologies. For this project, I collaborated as an external designer rather than as a club member.

The poster features a long object with a light green gradient as the main visual element, which was consistently extended across various printed materials to create a cohesive visual system.
